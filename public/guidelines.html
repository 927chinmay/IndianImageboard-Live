Community Guidelines

Our Goal: To provide a clean, safe, and engaging platform for discussion, open to everyone. To achieve this, we require all users to adhere to the following rules.

  • 1. Be Respectful

    Do not personally attack, threaten, harass, or insult other users. Criticize ideas, not people.

  • 2. No Hate Speech

    Content that promotes discrimination, bigotry, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, or sexual orientation is strictly forbidden.

  • 3. No Illegal Content

    Do not post or link to anything that violates Indian law. This includes, but is not limited to, copyrighted material you don't own, and content related to illegal activities.

  • 4. Keep it Clean

    No posting of pornographic, excessively violent, or graphically disturbing content.

  • 5. No Spam or Self-Promotion

    Do not use the platform for unsolicited advertising or commercial self-promotion.

  • 6. Respect Privacy

    Do not post the private information of any individual without their explicit consent. This is known as "doxxing" and will result in an immediate ban.

  • 7. Post to the Correct Board

    Please post your content in the most relevant discussion board to keep conversations organized.
